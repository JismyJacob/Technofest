Technofest

Technofest is a dynamic and inclusive tech event designed to bring together innovative minds, passionate coders, curious learners, and future tech leaders. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a tech enthusiast, Technofest offers a platform to explore, compete, collaborate, and grow. From high-energy hackathons and blind coding challenges to thought-provoking tech quizzes — every event at Techmantra is curated to ignite creativity, sharpen skills, and build lasting connections. Technofest isn't just about competition — it's about inspiration, learning, and community. Join us to showcase your talent, learn from the best, and become part of a growing movement driving technology forward.

Events

Hackathon

A Hackathon is an exciting, time-bound event where individuals or teams come together to develop innovative tech-based solutions to real-world problems.

Blind Coding

Blind Coding is a fun and challenging coding event where participants are required to write code without seeing what they’re typing—the screen is either hidden, turned off, or disabled in some way. The goal is to test a programmer’s muscle memory, syntax knowledge, and logical thinking.

Tech Quiz

The Tech Quiz is an exciting competition designed to test your knowledge of technology, programming, computer science, and the latest industry trends. Participants will face a series of challenging questions covering topics such as coding languages, software tools, tech history, innovations, and popular gadgets.
